## Disable and Uninstall All Mobile Browsers

- **Uninstall All Browsers:**  
  Go to **Settings > Apps > [Browser Name] > Uninstall** to remove browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave, etc.

- **Disable All System Browsers:**  
  For system apps (e.g., Chrome on Android), go to **Settings > Apps > Chrome > Disable** to prevent them from running.

- **Force Stop All Browsers:**  
  Navigate to **Settings > Apps > [Browser Name] > Force Stop** to terminate any currently running browser processes.
